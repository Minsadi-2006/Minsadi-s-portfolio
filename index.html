My Projects

Make a Propeller Car with DC Motor

This project involves building a simple car powered by a DC motor and a propeller. The car is lightweight and can move forward when the motor spins the propeller, creating thrust. It’s a fun experiment in basic mechanics and electronics.

Automated Pedestrian Traffic Control System

An intelligent traffic control system designed to detect pedestrians at crosswalks and automatically manage traffic lights to ensure their safety. The system uses sensors and cameras to identify pedestrian presence and optimizes traffic flow accordingly.

Water Tank Filtering System

An automated water filtering system designed for household water tanks. It uses sensors to detect water quality and triggers filtration processes to ensure clean water supply.
